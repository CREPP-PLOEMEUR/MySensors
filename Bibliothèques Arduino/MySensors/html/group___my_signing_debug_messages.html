Explanation of the abstract signing related debug messages.

MySigning-related log messages, format: [!]SYSTEM:SUB SYSTEM:MESSAGE

  • [!] Exclamation mark is prepended in case of error or warning
  • SYSTEM:
    • SGN messages emitted by MySigning
  • SUB SYSTEMS:
    • SGN:INI from signerInit
    • SGN:PER from signerInit
    • SGN:PRE from signerPresentation
    • SGN:SGN from signerSignMsg
    • SGN:VER from signerVerifyMsg
    • SGN:SKP from signerSignMsg or signerVerifyMsg (skipSign)
    • SGN:NCE from signerProcessInternal (signerInternalProcessNonceRequest)
    • SGN:BND from the signing backends
